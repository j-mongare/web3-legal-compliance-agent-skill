# Howey Test Quick Reference Checklist

## 1. Investment of Money
- Cash, crypto, or other consideration contributed?
- Expectation of return?

## 2. Common Enterprise
- Horizontal commonality (pooling of funds)?
- Vertical commonality (reliance on promoter)?

## 3. Expectation of Profit
- Marketing of passive returns / APY / yield?
- Revenue sharing or buyback mechanisms?
- Appreciation driven by others' efforts?

## 4. Efforts of Others
- Managerial / entrepreneurial efforts by promoters?
- Are managerial/enterprenurial functions discretionary?
- Governance concentration or central control?

**Instruction:** Always document evidence **for** and **against** each prong. Never give a definitive "is/is not a security" conclusion.
